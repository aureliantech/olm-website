What a TMB complaint triggers for Dallas healthcare professionals.

The Texas Medical Board investigates complaints against physicians statewide, while the Texas Board of Nursing handles CNM and nurse practitioner matters. Both operate on their own timelines — and both start building their records before you have a formal opportunity to respond. The initial written response to the board is the most consequential document in your defense. A passive response tells the board the case is uncontested. An evidence-anchored counter-response built on a parallel investigation forces a dismissal decision before a hearing is ever scheduled.
