Serving San Antonio Physicians

A TMB complaint doesn't pause your schedule. Neither does our response.

A Texas Medical Board complaint triggers a process that moves on the board's timeline — not yours. From the day you receive notice, the board is building its investigative record. Every day without an active defense strategy is a day the board builds unopposed.

The Law Firm of Octavia LaVon Martinez PLLC serves San Antonio physicians through a fully virtual model — no travel, no delays. You receive the same aggressive, dismissal-focused Counter Protocol representation as any client, activated immediately upon retention.

The initial response window is the most critical phase.

Most physicians receive a TMB complaint notice and spend several days deciding what to do. That time costs you. The initial written response to the board is the most consequential document in your defense — and a passive response allows the board to set the narrative unchallenged.

An evidence-anchored counter-response, filed with urgency, forces a dismissal decision. That window does not reopen. Call us today and we will assess exactly where you stand.
