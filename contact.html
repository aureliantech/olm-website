Strategy Sessions
Paid strategy sessions only

This firm does not offer free strategy sessions. The initial strategy session is a paid service — one hour of real legal analysis applied to your specific situation. You leave knowing exactly where you stand and what your options are. The session is not a commitment to further representation.

Confidentiality
Treat your initial inquiry carefully

While your inquiry is treated with confidentiality from the first contact, attorney-client privilege is formally established through a retainer agreement. Please do not include sensitive, confidential, or incriminating details in online inquiries before privilege has been established. A phone call or the consultation itself is the appropriate place for those details.

Response Time
Online booking is immediate

Booking online is the fastest way to secure a strategy session time — confirmation comes immediately with no waiting period. For urgent matters where timing is critical, note the urgency in your booking and it will be addressed. Phone calls during business hours are answered directly when available.
