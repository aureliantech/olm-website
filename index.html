Meet Octavia L. Martinez

Biography & Background 01

A former felony prosecutor and graduate of The University of Virginia and The Ohio State University Moritz College of Law, Octavia returned to the practice of law in 2024 after a season of staying at home with her children on the family homestead.

She reactivated her license not for routine, but to intervene in a high-stakes crisis.

She now challenges institutional power with necessary force — defending independent practitioners against regulatory overreach, resolving complex probate failures, fighting medical negligence, protecting free speech, and vindicating victims of slander and libel.

Our Process

How we work.

We utilize a structured intake process to ensure every case is evaluated with precision before formal retention.

01
First Contact
Intake Call

Your initial contact is screened by our intake team to ensure your matter falls within our practice areas. This ensures we are the right fit for your needs from the start.

02
Strategy
Paid Consultation

We conduct a paid, confidential consultation to discuss your specific situation. This is a working session where we analyze your objectives and outline the legal landscape.

Fee applies
03
Analysis
Initial Investigation

For litigation, malpractice, and complex defense matters. Before filing suit or fully engaging in defense, we may require a paid file review. Fee applies
04
Engagement
Retainer & Onboarding

Once a strategy is agreed upon, we execute a formal representation agreement and collect the retainer. From this moment, Octavia Martinez Law is your dedicated counsel, and we begin the work of securing your outcome.
